What is two-factor authentication?

Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of security to your online accounts. Verifying your identity using a second factor (like your phone or other device) prevents anyone but you from logging in, even if they know your password.

Why is the University requiring me to use two-factor?

You have been identified as a staff or faculty member with access to critical student information or other University systems that are targets for phishing and social engineering scams.

Two-factor authentication helps protect our students, faculty and staff from identity theft and University systems from being hacked.
